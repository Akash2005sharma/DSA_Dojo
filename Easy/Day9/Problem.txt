Your task is to calculate the number of unique rings comprising n gems, each available in 
m different hues. Rings are deemed distinct if they cannot be rotated to align with each other.
Your objective is to determine the total count of such distinct rings possible under these specifications.

Input
The only input line has two numbers n and m: the number of gems and hues.
Output
Print one integer: the number of different rings modulo 10^9+7.

Constraints:
1 <= n,m <=10^6
