Luna is traversing Mistland on a misty day with 'a' units of fuel. Each unit enables her to travel a distance of 1 unit.
Determine the total number of cities she can reach given her current location and the interconnected city layout.

The opening line includes four integers:
n: denoting the quantity of cities in Mistland.
k: representing the count of roads linking various cities in Mistland.
a: the remaining fuel units in Luna's vehicle.
c: Luna's current city.
The subsequent m lines delineate the road connections between cities as pairs of integers u,v, signifying a two-way road between cities u and v.
